Mandelbrot Set Viewer - 2018 (C++, SDL2)

This seemed like a great computing related application for complex numbers when I was doing further mathematics AS. It turned out to be relatively simple to implement since it uses an iterative formula. However, scaling the viewer and allowing panning was a task that took much longer than I'd initially anticipated. It was also a good opportunity to try my hand at multi-threading because this was a perfect example of a SIMD problem.
